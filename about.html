About My Insurance Calc

Our Mission

My Insurance Calc was built with one goal: make insurance math accessible to everyone. Insurance is one of the most important financial decisions most people make — yet the tools to understand it are often buried behind sales funnels, paywalls, or jargon-heavy PDFs.

We built this site to change that. Every calculator on My Insurance Calc is free, requires no signup, and runs entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

What We Cover

We cover the full range of personal insurance needs — life insurance, disability, health insurance for the self-employed, renters and home insurance, pet insurance, auto and SR-22, EV insurance, umbrella coverage, and more. New tools and guides are added regularly based on what people are actually searching for.

Beyond calculators, our blog covers the situations that standard insurance guides ignore: getting life insurance after a cancer diagnosis, navigating SR-22 requirements after a DUI, finding affordable coverage as a freelancer or gig worker, and understanding the fine print that affects your actual payout.

How We Build Our Tools

Every calculator on this site is built using publicly available actuarial data, industry rate surveys, and regulatory filings. We cross-reference multiple sources to ensure our estimates reflect real-world pricing as closely as possible. Our tools are designed to give you a realistic starting point — not a sales pitch.

We update our tools regularly to reflect changes in industry averages, regulatory updates (such as ACA guidelines and FPL thresholds), and shifts in market pricing.

Editorial Standards

All articles on this site are researched and written to provide accurate, unbiased information. We do not accept payment to feature specific insurers or products. When we mention insurance companies, it is for informational purposes only — not as a paid endorsement.

We are not a licensed insurance agency and do not sell insurance. Our content is intended to help you understand your options so you can make informed decisions with the help of a licensed professional.
